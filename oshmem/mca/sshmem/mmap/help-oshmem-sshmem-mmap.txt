[mmap:out-of-memory]
'sshmem' 'mmap' was failed to allocate shared memory segment
of requested size. Returned error corresponds to
out of memory case.

Try decreasing symmetric heap area size with
'-x SHMEM_SYMMETRIC_HEAP_SIZE=<value>'.
